Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Become handles information when you use the daily mindset and Signals app.

Effective date: July 22, 2026
Last updated: July 22, 2026

ASIOS LLC ("ASIOS," "we," "our," or "us") provides Become. This Privacy Policy applies to Become and its related support and paid features.

1. Become and Local Content

Become is a daily mindset and Signals app. It is designed to be local-first. User-created Signals, saved Signals, marked Signals, Vault and history items, My Signals, preferences, reminder settings, widget preferences, and custom photo backgrounds are stored locally on your device.

ASIOS does not receive, store, or review local Signal text or custom photo backgrounds through Become's core app features. Become has no account system, and it does not provide backend or cloud synchronization for this core app content. You are responsible for securing your device and any device backups.

2. Device Permissions

Photo library. Become requests photo library access only if you choose a custom image background. The selected image remains local to your device for that feature. Become V1 provides no in-app image capture feature.

Notifications. If you enable notifications, Become may use Apple device notification infrastructure to provide local reminders. Reminder preferences are stored on your device.

Location. Become does not request or collect precise or coarse location permission.

3. Information Processed Outside Local Content

To operate subscriptions, paywalls, analytics, and advertising measurement, ASIOS and the providers described below may process limited information such as subscription status, product identifiers, anonymous or vendor identifiers, device and technical information, app version, operating system version, app interactions, paywall interactions, and purchase, conversion, trial, or subscription events.

Become does not intentionally send saved Signal text, local custom photos, your name, email address, phone number, health information, financial information, or location in analytics or advertising-measurement events.

4. Purchases and Paid Access

Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store and StoreKit using your Apple Account. ASIOS does not receive your full payment-card information. Apple provides purchase information needed for the App Store transaction, and we use subscription and entitlement information to provide paid access and restore purchases.

5. Service Providers

Become uses the following providers for the stated purposes:

  • Apple App Store, StoreKit, and Apple Account: app distribution, purchase processing, and purchase restoration.
  • RevenueCat: subscription and entitlement management.
  • Superwall: paywall presentation and measurement.
  • PostHog: product analytics used to understand app usage and improve Become. Session replay is disabled.
  • Apple AdServices: paid advertising attribution where enabled.
  • Firebase and Google Analytics 4: campaign attribution and conversion measurement where configured.
  • TikTok: campaign attribution and conversion measurement where configured.
  • Apple notification infrastructure: local and device notification behavior where applicable.

These providers process information under their own terms and privacy practices. Their processing may include information needed to provide their services, such as technical identifiers, event information, or transaction and entitlement status.

6. App Tracking Transparency

Become requests Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission before enabling external advertising-measurement exports that require tracking permission. You may deny this permission without losing Become functionality. You can manage this permission in iOS Settings. When tracking permission is denied, Become does not enable external advertising-measurement exports that depend on that permission.

7. How We Use and Share Information

We use the limited information processed outside local content to provide paid access, present and measure paywalls, understand app performance, measure advertising campaigns where enabled, maintain security, respond to support requests, and meet legal obligations.

We may share that limited information with the providers identified above, when you direct or consent to sharing, to comply with applicable law or valid legal process, to protect ASIOS or others, or in connection with a business transaction such as a merger or acquisition. ASIOS does not sell your personal information.

8. Retention and Deletion

Local content remains on your device until you delete it, delete the app, or your device otherwise removes it, subject to device backup and operating system behavior. We and our providers retain subscription, entitlement, paywall, analytics, advertising-measurement, and technical information only as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy and subject to applicable law and provider retention practices.

We cannot remotely delete content that exists only on your device. To request assistance with personal information processed by ASIOS, contact us at support@asios.tech.

9. Security, Children, and International Processing

We use reasonable safeguards for information processed by us, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Become is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ASIOS is a United States company, and providers may process limited information in the United States and other countries as permitted by applicable law.

10. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of covered personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, or to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. You may also manage photo library, notification, and App Tracking Transparency permissions in iOS Settings. Contact us to make a request; we may need to verify it and may retain information where required or permitted by law.

11. Changes and Contact

We may update this policy as Become or applicable requirements change. We will post the updated policy with a revised date and provide additional notice where required.

Questions or privacy requests may be sent to ASIOS LLC at support@asios.tech.

Your use of Become is also subject to the Become Terms and Conditions.
