Privacy Policy

ASIOS LLC values your privacy. We collect and use personal information only as needed to deliver our products, services, websites, and mobile applications, and to communicate with you about them (collectively, our “Services”).

Your personal information may include:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Telephone number
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Billing and payment information
  • Candidate information (for job applicants)
  • Other data that could directly or indirectly identify you

This Privacy Policy explains how and why we use your personal information, as well as how you can access, update, and control your data. If you have questions, you may contact our Data Protection Officer (“DPO”) at: ed@asios.tech.

Information We Collect and How We Collect It

We collect personal information when you:

  • Create an account or purchase Services
  • Connect our Services to third-party accounts
  • Request customer support
  • Complete forms, sign up for newsletters, or download resources
  • Participate in surveys, contests, or promotions
  • Apply for employment

We also use cookies, analytics tools, log files, metadata, device identifiers, and performance-data tracking to enhance and secure our Services.

How We Use Information

We limit our use of your information to what is necessary to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services
  • Improve, personalize, and expand the Services
  • Understand and analyze how you use the Services
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • Contact you about your account or support requests
  • Meet legal, compliance, and regulatory obligations

How We Secure, Store, and Retain Data

We follow industry-standard security practices, including encryption where appropriate. We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Services and for legitimate legal or business purposes.

How to Access, Update, or Delete Your Data

To access or update your personal information, sign in to your account and visit your settings page.

To delete or export your personal data, submit a request to ed@asios.tech.

GDPR Compliance (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or EEA, you are entitled to the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access – Request copies of your data.
  • Right to Rectification – Correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”).
  • Right to Restrict Processing.
  • Right to Object to Processing, including marketing.
  • Right to Data Portability – Obtain a copy in machine-readable format.
  • Right Not to Be Subject to Automated Decision-Making.

To exercise GDPR rights, email: ed@asios.tech.

If you believe we have not handled your request appropriately, you may file a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

Age Restrictions

Our Services are available only to individuals age 18 or older. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
