Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 27, 2025 — ASIOS LLC

ASIOS LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy is intended to apply across our portfolio of applications and services. Some parts of this policy may apply only to certain apps, features, or regions. If a specific application provides additional privacy disclosures or notices, those disclosures supplement this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope and Acceptance

By using our Services, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on the app, feature set, and how you use the Services, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and profile information: Name, username, email address, authentication identifiers, profile details, and other information you provide when creating or managing an account.
  • Authentication information: Information needed to sign in, including credentials you provide directly or identifiers returned by third-party sign-in providers such as Apple or Google.
  • User content: Photos, images, videos, text, prompts, messages, documents, uploads, logs, and other content you submit through the Services.
  • App activity and usage data: Screens viewed, features used, session information, interactions, and timestamps.
  • Device and technical data: Device model, operating system version, app version, IP address, language, time zone, browser type, and similar technical information.
  • Diagnostics and crash data: Crash logs, error reports, performance traces, and related troubleshooting data.
  • Purchase and subscription information: Information related to subscriptions, purchases, billing status, renewal status, transaction identifiers, and entitlement state. Payment credentials are generally handled by Apple, Google, Stripe, or another payment processor, not stored directly by us unless explicitly stated.
  • Push notification data: Device tokens, notification preferences, and related delivery metadata.
  • Customer support and communications: Information you provide when contacting us, submitting feedback, or communicating with us.
  • Sensitive information: Some apps may process health, wellness, nutrition, or other sensitive information that you choose to provide. Where required, we provide additional disclosures or obtain consent.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you: When you create an account, sign in, upload content, make a purchase, contact support, enable features, or otherwise provide information.
  • Automatically: Through app operation, logs, analytics tools, crash reporting tools, cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies.
  • From third parties: From authentication providers, app stores, payment processors, analytics vendors, cloud hosting providers, or integrations you choose to connect.

4. How We Use Information

We may use information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Create and manage user accounts and enable sign-in.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Process purchases, subscriptions, renewals, refunds, and entitlement checks.
  • Store and sync user data through our backend systems where applicable.
  • Deliver push notifications, reminders, service alerts, and product updates.
  • Analyze usage trends and feature performance.
  • Monitor crashes, debug errors, and improve reliability and security.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with users.
  • Detect fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, or other harmful activity.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.
  • Provide AI-powered or automated features requested by the user.

5. Accounts, Sign-In, and Authentication

Some of our Services allow you to create an account and sign in. When you do so, we may collect account details, identifiers, and related authentication data. Some apps may also support third-party sign-in providers such as Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, or similar services. In those cases, we receive information made available by the provider, subject to the provider’s permissions and your choices.

6. Purchases, Billing, and Subscriptions

Some Services may offer paid subscriptions, in-app purchases, one-time purchases, or premium features. Purchases may be processed by Apple, Google, Stripe, or another payment processor. We may receive transaction metadata such as purchase status, subscription tier, renewal status, billing country, and transaction identifiers in order to activate and manage access to paid features. We generally do not store full payment card details unless explicitly stated.

7. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may use analytics providers and crash reporting tools to understand how users interact with the Services, diagnose failures, improve performance, and prioritize product improvements. This may include identifiers, device information, session information, feature usage, and crash diagnostics.

8. Backend Storage and Cloud Features

Some apps store user data locally on-device, while others may store or sync data through our backend systems or cloud infrastructure. Where backend storage is used, we may store account data, user-generated content, app settings, subscription state, and related operational data as needed to provide the relevant functionality.

9. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we may collect and use device tokens, notification preferences, and related delivery metadata to send transactional messages, reminders, alerts, feature updates, or marketing notifications where permitted by law. You can usually manage notification preferences in your device settings or within the app.

10. AI Features and Processing of User Content

Some of our Services include AI-powered features. Depending on the app, these features may process user-submitted content such as photos, images, audio, video, text prompts, documents, logs, or other inputs in order to provide the requested output, recommendation, analysis, classification, transformation, or automation.

When AI features are used:

  • User content may be transmitted to our secure backend systems and, where necessary, to trusted third-party AI providers.
  • Content is processed only to provide the requested feature, maintain the service, or troubleshoot technical problems.
  • We do not use user-submitted content to train our own AI models.
  • We do not permit third-party AI providers to use user-submitted content for their model training unless we explicitly disclose that and obtain any required consent.
  • If a particular feature processes content only on-device, that feature may not send the content to our servers for that operation.

11. Service Providers and Third Parties

We may use trusted third-party service providers to help us operate the Services. Depending on the app, these may include providers for:

  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure
  • Authentication and identity management
  • Payment processing and subscription management
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Push notification delivery
  • Customer support and communications
  • Artificial intelligence and content processing
  • App distribution through Apple App Store, Google Play, or similar platforms

These providers receive only the information reasonably necessary to perform services on our behalf.

12. How We May Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers: To operate, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • With integrated third parties: When you choose to use a third-party sign-in method or connect a third-party service.
  • For legal reasons: To comply with law, regulation, legal process, or valid government requests.
  • To protect rights and safety: To protect our users, our Services, our company, or the public.
  • In business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, sale of assets, or similar transaction.
  • With your consent: Where you direct us to share information.
  • In aggregated or de-identified form: For analytics, reporting, product planning, or similar business purposes.

We do not sell personal information for money. If any app engages in advertising, data sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising, or similar processing, we will provide additional disclosures where required by law.

13. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, maintain accounts, support subscriptions, fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and protect the Services.

Retention periods may vary depending on the app, feature, legal requirements, and the type of data involved. We may delete, anonymize, or aggregate information when it is no longer needed.

14. Security

15. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Where required by law, we implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

16. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to investigate and delete the data where required.
