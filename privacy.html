Privacy Policy

How ASIOS LLC handles information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect information when you use our websites, applications, and related services.

Effective date: May 26, 2026
Last updated: May 26, 2026

ASIOS LLC ("ASIOS," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, our mobile applications, including Become and Faro, and related support and paid features (collectively, the "Services").

This policy describes current practices for the Services. If we introduce an account system, cloud synchronization, Android distribution, advertising or tracking SDKs, remote content processing, or materially different applications, we will update applicable disclosures before those practices apply.

1. Current Applications and Local Content Processing

Become is an inspiration and motivation quote application. It may allow you to use the camera or choose an image from your photo library as a background for quotes. Faro is a recurring expense and subscription organization application. It may allow you to select images of documentation and use Apple's on-device text-recognition capabilities to help enter recurring expense information.

For the current versions of Become and Faro:

  • Text you enter in Faro and images you select or capture in either app for core functionality remain locally on your device.
  • Text recognized from documentation in Faro is processed and stored locally on your device.
  • ASIOS does not receive, store, or review those images, recognized text, or user-entered local records through the core app features.
  • We do not use that locally stored content to train artificial intelligence models.

You control local content through the applicable app and your device. Deleting content in the app, where offered, or deleting the app from your device removes locally stored app content, subject to device backups or operating system behavior outside our control.

2. Information We Collect or Process

Although core user content remains on-device, we and our service providers may process limited information necessary to operate, improve, and monetize the Services:

  • Purchase and subscription information: Product identifiers, subscription or purchase status, trial status, entitlement state, renewal or expiration information, and transaction-related identifiers required to present paid offerings and provide premium access.
  • App usage and analytics information: App launches, sessions, feature interactions, screen or event information captured through configured analytics events, and similar product usage information. We do not intentionally include your locally stored images, recognized text, or expense records in analytics events.
  • Device and technical information: App version, operating system version, device model, locale, region, time zone, network or IP-derived region information, vendor or anonymous identifiers, SDK version, and similar technical information.
  • Paywall interaction information: Paywall views, offering selection, conversion events, trial starts, purchase events, restore events, and subscription status used to display and measure paid offerings.
  • Push notification information: Device token, notification authorization state, preferences, and delivery-related information if you enable notifications.
  • Support information: Your email address and any app name, device information, description, attachment, or other information you choose to provide when contacting us.
  • Website technical information: Standard connection information may be processed by our website hosting or font delivery providers when you visit our website, such as IP address, browser and device information, and request information.

Our current applications do not require an ASIOS user account or sign-in.

3. Device Permissions

A Service may request device permissions only when needed for the relevant feature:

  • Camera and photo library: Become uses these permissions so you can select or capture quote backgrounds. Faro uses photo access so you can select documentation for local text recognition.
  • Notifications: If enabled, notifications may be used for reminders or app-related messages.

You can manage device permissions through your device settings. Declining an optional permission may limit the associated feature, but does not prevent you from using unrelated app functionality.

4. How We Use Information

We use information collected outside local-only content processing to:

  • Operate and provide the Services and their paid features.
  • Present paywalls, process subscription entitlements, and restore purchases.
  • Understand use of the apps, evaluate performance, and improve functionality and usability.
  • Send notifications you enable and support app reminders where applicable.
  • Respond to support requests and feedback.
  • Detect misuse, troubleshoot errors, maintain security, and meet legal obligations.

5. Purchases and Subscriptions

Our iOS applications may offer auto-renewable weekly, monthly, or yearly subscriptions, free trials where displayed, and lifetime access offerings. Purchases made in our iOS applications are processed by Apple through the App Store. ASIOS does not receive your full payment card information.

We use purchase and entitlement information received through Apple and our subscription service providers to determine whether you are entitled to premium features, present appropriate offerings, and support purchase restoration. For cancellation, refund, and payment-account management, use Apple's applicable App Store or Apple Account services.

6. Service Providers and Disclosures

We use service providers that process limited information on our behalf or as needed to provide platform functionality. Our current app service providers include:

  • Apple: App distribution, in-app purchase processing, device permissions, push notification infrastructure, and Apple's on-device frameworks used by the apps.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and entitlement management. RevenueCat processes purchase history and related app-user or device identifiers needed to manage paid access.
  • Superwall: Paywall presentation and measurement. Superwall may process purchase information, paywall events, anonymous app-user or vendor identifiers, and device or technical attributes used for paywall functionality and analytics.
  • PostHog: Product analytics used to understand app usage and improve the Services. We use event autocapture for configured interaction analytics. Session replay is disabled in Become and Faro. Analytics configuration must not transmit your locally stored photos, recognized text, or expense records.

Our website also uses third-party hosting and Google Fonts delivery, which may process ordinary network request information when the website loads.

We require service providers acting on our behalf to handle information consistently with their responsibilities and applicable law. Their own privacy terms also govern their processing:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Superwall Privacy Policy
  • PostHog Privacy Notice
  • Google Privacy Policy

7. How We Share Information

We may share information described in this policy:

  • With service providers described above to provide analytics, subscription management, paywall functionality, notifications, hosting, and support for the Services.
  • At your direction or with your consent.
  • To comply with law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request.
  • To protect the rights, property, security, or safety of ASIOS, our users, or others.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or transfer of all or part of our business, subject to applicable legal protections.

We do not sell your personal information. Our current applications do not share user data for cross-context behavioral advertising and do not use third-party advertising or advertising-tracking SDKs. If this changes, we will update this policy and obtain consent or device-level tracking permission where required before enabling such practices.

8. Analytics Consent and Your Choices

Where required by applicable law or platform policy, we request consent before collecting analytics or similar usage information. You may manage device permissions in your device settings and may contact us about privacy choices at support@asios.tech.

Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission will be requested before tracking across other companies' apps or websites if any future feature requires such tracking. Current Services are not intended to perform that tracking.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local content: Images, recognized text, and expense or app content stored locally in Become or Faro remain on your device until you delete the content, delete the app, or your device otherwise removes it.
  • Subscription and operational information: We and our service providers retain purchase, entitlement, analytics, paywall, technical, and notification information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, analyze performance, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements, subject to provider retention practices and applicable law.
  • Support correspondence: We retain support communications for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain support records, and meet legal obligations.

To request deletion of personal information processed by ASIOS or request assistance concerning provider-associated information, contact support@asios.tech. We may need to verify your request and may retain information where required or permitted by law. We cannot remotely delete content that exists only on your device.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect information processed by us. Keeping core app content on-device limits transmission of that content to ASIOS, but no device, service, or method of transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your device and device backups.

11. International Processing

ASIOS is a United States company and our service providers may process information in the United States and other countries. Where required for international transfers of personal information, we and our service providers use legally recognized safeguards.

12. Children

The Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, contact us at support@asios.tech so we can take appropriate action.
