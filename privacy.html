Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 27, 2025 — ASIOS LLC

ASIOS LLC ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and retain information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Scope and Acceptance

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

Information We May Collect

Depending on the specific app or feature you use, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: Information you provide when contacting us, such as your email address.
  • App content: Photos, images, text inputs, or other content you choose to submit when using app features.
  • Usage and diagnostic data: Technical and operational data such as device type, operating system version, app version, timestamps, crash information, and similar diagnostic information.
  • Support and feedback information: Information you provide in support requests, feedback forms, or other correspondence.

How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you: When you contact us, send feedback, or submit content through app features.
  • Automatically: Through app operation, diagnostics, and service logs needed to maintain and improve the Services.
  • From service providers: When trusted providers help us operate app functionality.

How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Services.
  • Process your requests and deliver app features.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and usability.
  • Respond to support requests and feedback.
  • Protect the security and integrity of the Services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Photo Processing and AI Features

Some features may allow you to submit photos, images, or text for processing. For example, in apps such as CalTrack, user-selected meal photos or meal descriptions may be sent to our secure backend services and, where needed, to trusted third-party AI providers solely to perform the requested analysis or generate the requested output.

  • Submitted content is transmitted over secure channels.
  • Content is used only to provide the requested feature or result.
  • We do not use submitted content to train third-party AI models unless explicitly stated otherwise and agreed to by you.
  • We retain submitted content only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the feature, troubleshoot issues, meet legal obligations, or protect the Services.

If a feature processes content entirely on-device, no such content is sent to our servers for that feature.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party service providers to help operate the Services. Depending on the app and feature set, these providers may include:

  • Apple, for App Store distribution and related platform services.
  • AI service providers, such as OpenAI, when required to power app features that analyze or transform submitted content.

These providers receive only the information reasonably necessary to perform their services on our behalf.

Data Storage and Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Services, fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

Some apps may also store user-generated content or settings locally on your device. For example, CalTrack stores meal logs, hydration data, and profile settings locally on-device unless otherwise stated.

Security

We use reasonable and industry-standard measures to protect information in transit and at rest where appropriate. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Links to Other Websites

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before interacting with them.
