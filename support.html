What to include

When contacting support, include the app name, your device model, iOS version, app version if available, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots are helpful when they do not include private information.

Purchases and subscriptions

iOS purchases and subscriptions are managed through Apple. If you need to cancel a subscription, request a refund, or update payment information, use your Apple Account or Apple Support.
